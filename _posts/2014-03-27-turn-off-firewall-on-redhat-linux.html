--- layout: post title: Turn off the firewall on RedHat linux date: '2014-03-27T21:06:00.000Z' author: David tags: modified_time: '2014-03-27T21:08:26.065Z' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2027514548288128942.post-7640093124386117990 blogger_orig_url: http://davidkerwick.blogspot.com/2014/03/turn-off-firewall-on-redhat-linux.html --- If you are  having problems connecting with the Weblogic server you have just installed it's worth looking at the firewall, as every good developer knows all problems are either the network or the firewall ;)

service iptables stop
chkconfig iptables off

service ip6tables stop
chkconfig ip6tables off


A total sledgehammer approach and only for dev machines, I'll come back and work out just the ports and ip's I need in the future... honestly
